About Us

Yog Mantra is a yoga and fitness organization founded in 2013 by Manjunath Poojary and Sujatha Poojary in Mumbai, Maharashtra. We have been serving clients with yoga and fitness classes. We started with a yoga studio and offline classes and now we have spread across both online and offline modes of training. We have served over 200 clients and have 10+ years of experience in yoga training.
